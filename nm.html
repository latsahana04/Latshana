English Wikipedia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Overview

The English Wikipedia is the primary English-language edition of Wikipedia. It was launched on 15 January 2001 by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger.

History

The English edition was the first to be created and has since grown to become one of the largest collections of free knowledge in history.

By 2004, it had surpassed 250,000 articles, and by 2023 it had over 6.5 million articles covering a vast range of topics from historical events to scientific discoveries.

Statistics

  • Launch date: January 15, 2001
  • Number of articles: 6,583,000+
  • Active editors: 40,000+
  • Total edits: Over 1 billion

Notable Facts

Wikipedia has been translated into more than 300 languages, making it one of the most widely read and collaborative projects in history.
